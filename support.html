FAQ

How do I restore my Pro purchase?
Open the app, go to the Pro / store screen, and tap “Restore Purchases.”

How do I reset or manage notifications?
Manage reminders in your device’s Settings > Notifications > Mindlapse.

How is my data handled?
See our Privacy Policy.
